Tanika Green - Celebrity Makeup Artist

Tanika Green is a freelancing makeup artist and image consultant. Her love for the fashion and beauty industries led her to pursue studies at Robert Fiance Makeup Academy in New Jersey. Since graduating, she has dedicated all of her time, energy and resources into her career.

In the past, Tanika has worked as a makeup artist on the set of numerous movies, photo shoots and video shoots. In addition to freelancing for New York Fashion Week (NYFW) and Jersey City Fashion Week (JCFW) Tanika has also had the opportunity to work with Rosie Perez , Whoopi Goldberg's daughter Alex Martin and the Whoopi Goldberg production, TONY Award winning actress and entertainer Melba Moore and TONY Award winning playwright Ntozake Shange, and reality star Drea from "Basketball Wives." Tanika has also had her work featured in ESSENCE Magazine, SHEEN Magazine and BLACKMEN Magazine. Her artisttry has graced run was such as movies James Brown movie premiere as well as 2016 Black Girls Rock.

She is committed to enhancing the outer beauty of each client to mirror or reflect their hidden inner beauty. She prides herself on maintaining a professional image, quality customer care and noteworthy service which she believes has paved the way for past opportunities and will continue to open doors for future success.
