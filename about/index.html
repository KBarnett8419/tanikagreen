Tanika Green: Celebrity Makeup Artist

Tanika Green is a celebrity makeup artist and image consultant. Her love for the fashion and beauty industries led her to pursue studies at Robert Fiance Makeup Academy in New Jersey. Since graduating, she has dedicated all of her time, energy and resources into her career.

In the past, Tanika has worked as a makeup artist on the set of numerous photo shoots and video shoots. In addition to servicing New York Fashion Week (NYFW) and Jersey City Fashion Week (JCFW) Tanika has also had the opportunity to work with Rosie Perez, Alex Martin of Whoopi Goldberg Productions, TONY Award winning actress and entertainer Melba Moore, TONY Award winning playwright Ntozake Shange, comedian W.Kamu Bell, and reality star Drea from "Basketball Wives." Tanika has also had her work featured in ESSENCE Magazine, SHEEN Magazine and BLACKMEN Magazine. She has even done the makeup of Tomi-Rae Brown, widow of the late James Brown for the movie premiere of "Get On Up."

She is committed to enhancing the outer beauty of each client to mirror their inner beauty. She prides herself on maintaining a professional image, quality customer care and noteworthy service which has paved the way for past opportunities and will continue to open doors for future success.
